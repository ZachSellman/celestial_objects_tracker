Welcome to Celestial.OT

A free web app built with Python utilizing Django, Selenium, and more!

How does this work?

Celestial.OT shows you what celestial objects/phenomena are visible at your location

Just enter your city, and it'll handle the rest!

You can check out some cool stuff like this:

Jupiter

Jupiter is the fifth planet from our Sun and is, by far, the largest planet in the solar system; it's more than twice as massive as all the other planets combined!

Mars

Mars is the fourth planet from the sun and has a distinct rusty red appearance and two unusual moons. It's only about 10% as massive as Earth, and has no global magnetic field.

Neptune

Neptune is the most distant planet in the solar system and its orbit is at a distance of about 4.5 billion km from Earth. One orbit around the sun takes Neptune 164 years!

The Void of Space!

Technically this is always visible, even in the daylight. The other objects are definitely more interesting to look at, and they also don't terrify me.
